Our Story

Rooted in craft, growing at scale

TwoRoot Technologies is a software studio built by two brothers who genuinely love what they do. Ameh and Samuel started this because they believed great software should come from anywhere in the world, including Nigeria. Every project we take on gets our full attention, our honest thinking, and our best work.

The name says it all. Two roots, growing into something bigger together. We operate under Atsoel Prime Choice Limited and work with clients across Africa and beyond.

Work With Us

Let's build something together

We are genuinely excited to hear from people with interesting problems to solve. Whether you have a full brief or just a rough idea, reach out and we will have an honest conversation about what is possible.

We typically respond within 24 hours and we do not do pushy sales calls. Just a real chat about your project.

1

Say hello

Drop us a message through any of the channels on the right. Tell us a little about yourself and what you are working on. No formal brief needed at this stage.

2

We have a conversation

We will set up a short call or chat to understand your goals, your timeline and your budget. We ask honest questions and give you honest answers.

3

We send you a proposal

If we are a good fit, we put together a clear proposal covering scope, timeline and cost. No hidden fees, no vague estimates.

4

We get to work

Once you are happy, we kick things off. You stay in the loop every step of the way and we build together until it is something you are proud of.
